VICTORY FOUNDATION

Victory Foundation is a Non Profit Faith Based Organisation that aims to Nature, Protect and Empower Women, Children and Teenagers.

MISSION.

To eliminate stigma and discrimination through empowering and equipping the community in training and provision of physical, social, emotional and spiritual needs.


VISION.

A world with less suffering, changed and health community.

OUR CORE VALUES.
  • Respect of human dignity
  • Non-judgemental
  • Accountable
  • Team Work
  • Upholding of positive Family values, Rural areas, Poor/marginalized Populations-outreach is our Priority

OUR OBJECTIVES.
  • To build capacity of young people on health issues affecting them by facilitating their access to health infomation services.
  • To address violence against women including harmful traditional health practices.
  • To build powerful partnerships with innovative partners for the best interest of the young people, children and women
  • Discipline and mentor the children, youth and women
  • To provide supportive interventions for those infected, affected and vulnerable to HIV/AIDS and their care givers, in order to give them hope & reduce the risk of infection
